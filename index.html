About Conference


ICISGâ€™22 has emerged as an enduring techno-platform to connect education experts and passionate educators all over the world for improving the potential for excellence in engineering education. It provides a premier interdisciplinary forum for researchers, engineers and academicians to present and discuss the most recent trends, innovations, concerns, practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted for Sustainable development and Green Environment. The conference also focuses on mutually sharing the advances and innovative technologies for the scientists, scholars, engineers and students from different universities and industry to present ongoing research activities, focused on solutions to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and hence to foster collaborations among stakeholders and researchers from distinct universities, national laboratories, government funding bodies and the industry.
